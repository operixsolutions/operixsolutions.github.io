Attendance Policies
Mind Break Café — HR Guidelines
Check-In Rules
On Time (within 15 min)
No deduction. 15-minute grace period from shift start.
Late (16 min – 1h 59m)
Allowed with warning. Deduction: 30 SAR per hour (prorated).
Example: 45 min late = −22 SAR.
2+ Hours Late → ABSENT
Marked absent. Cannot check in until next scheduled shift.
Check-Out Rules
Valid Window
Check out any time up to 30 minutes after shift end.
Example: shift ends 5:00 PM → checkout by 5:30 PM.
Early Checkout = No Pay
Leaving before shift ends means that session earns nothing.
After 30-min Window = Blocked
Cannot check out. Contact your manager to resolve.
Absence Deductions
Consecutive absent scheduled work days trigger escalating penalties:
Days
Situation
Deduction
1
One absent day
−1 day
2
Two consecutive days (e.g. Sun + Mon)
−2.5 days
3+
Three or more consecutive days (Sun + Mon + Tue)
−30% salary
Note: Consecutive = back-to-back scheduled work days only. Off days and weekends do not break a streak.
How Salary is Calculated
Daily RateMonthly ÷ Working Days This Month
Hourly RateDaily Rate ÷ 8 hours
EarnedHours Worked × Hourly Rate
Net SalaryEarned − All Deductions
Late deduction: 30 SAR/hour prorated. Example: 45 min late = −22 SAR.
Location & GPS
Must Be On-Site
You must be within your assigned work area to check in or out. GPS dot turns green when in range.
GPS Tips
If GPS shows outside but you are inside, allow location permission and wait 10–15 seconds for signal to update.
